About Me

As is probably obvious by the name at the top of the page, my name is Mitch Slater. I am a graduate student in my first semester of the Computer Science Masters program at Utah State University. I am currently employed by the United States Air Force, who have kindly allowed me to further my education.

I grew up primarily in Syracuse, Utah. I attended Clearfield High School before graduating in 2012, following which I served a mission for the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints in the Philippines. Upon my return, I began attending Brigham Young University and graduated with a degree in Mechanical Engineering in 2020.

Partway through my studies at BYU, I married one of my good friends from high school, and we are enjoying working on our new house and learning and growing with our almost 1-year-old daughter.

I enjoy spending my free time (such as it is) hiking, fishing, camping, backpacking, watching and participating in sports, and playing video games.
